About US

Rajasthan is India's largest state by area, covering 342,239 square kilometers, which is 10.41% of India's total land area. Known as the "Land of Kings," it's renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and numerous historical forts and palaces. Rajasthan is located in northwestern India, bounded on the west and northwest by Pakistan and shares domestic borders with the states of Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat. With a land area of 342,239 sq km, Rajasthan is the largest state in India geographically.8 The state is divided into 33 districts and comprises nine regionsAjmer State, Hadoti, Dhundhar, Gorwar, Shekhawati, Mewar, Marwar, Vagad and Mewat.
